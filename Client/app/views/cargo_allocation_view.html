Can your convoy carry the weight?


Please enter the total weight that is being carried, along with the total count of each vehicle in the fleet.
You will retrieve a total weight carried for each vehicle starting with the vehicle that has the smallest carrying capacity and finishing with the vehicle that has the largest carrying capacity, in addition to any remainder weight that was not available to be carried by the fleet.
